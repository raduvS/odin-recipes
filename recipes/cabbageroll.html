Cabbage Roll Recipe


Description

Cabbage roll casserole is the saucy, satisfying 9x13 dish that will take your weeknight dinners from boring to bold.
Enjoy a casserole dish that combines seasoned ground beef, chopped cabbage, and fluffy white rice with tomato sauce and beef broth for
a wonderfully cozy bite. Learn how to make cabbage roll casserole, plus get tips on how to make it your own and what side dishes to pair it with for a fabulous meal.

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 3 Â½ pounds chopped cabbage
  • 1 (29 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 (14 ounce) cans beef broth

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot
    skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain and discard grease.

  3. Step 3

    4. Combine cabbage, tomato sauce, onion, rice, and salt in a large mixing bowl.
    Stir in cooked ground beef. Pour mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish, then pour beef broth over top.

  4. Step 4

    5. Cover and bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour. Stir, re-cover, and bake until
    cabbage is tender and rice is done, 20 to 30 minutes more.
